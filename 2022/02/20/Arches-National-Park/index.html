Arches National Park

Created: 2022-02-20
Edited: 2022-11-22
227 words, 1 min
#Essay

A few days before the start of the spring semester. My friends rent a car with me pay a visit to the Arches National Park near Salt Lake City. To be honest, this is the most impressive national park I have ever visited in recent years.

The first day of arrival was 9.00 p.m. Pack up. We entered the park immediately. Park at night. With the evening breeze, we sat on the hood of the car. Looking up, there is a sea of stars. I haven’t seen so many stars for many years. Or a galaxy. I quickly took out my mobile phone, but found that the hardware limited by the mobile phone could not shoot gorgeous stars. Here is the Milky Way taken by a friend with a SLR camera.

The next day, we got up early in the morning. Drive to a observation deck at the arch. After setting up my mobile phone, I finally photographed the sunrise slowly rising from the mountains. With the morning breeze, watching the blue sky gradually be rendered by the morning sunlight. We are all looking forward to the next trip.

The next journey is to go to different arches. Although it’s February. But there is still ice and snow on the road. The soil is mixed with snow. There is also a different feeling.
